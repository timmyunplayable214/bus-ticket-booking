## 🎫 How to Book a Ticket

The booking process follows a simple logic. You select your departure point, your destination, and the date of travel.

1. Open the application from your desktop icon.
2. Select the "Search" tab on the main screen.
3. Type your starting city and your destination city into the boxes.
4. Use the calendar tool to pick your travel date. 
5. Select "Find Buses" to see a list of current options.
6. Choose a bus from the list to view arrival times and pricing.
7. Click "Book Seat" for your preferred time.
8. Enter your passenger details into the fields provided.
9. Confirm your choice to finalize the request.

## 💾 Managing Your Bookings

The software stores your travel history so you can review it later. Access the "My Trips" section from the top menu bar to see your current and past ticket numbers. If you need to print a ticket, look for the printer icon after selecting a specific trip. You can also export details to a PDF file if you prefer to keep a digital copy on your phone.

## 🛡️ Privacy and Data

This application keeps your travel data on your local hard drive. It only sends information to the server when you click the button to book a seat. Your payment details remain encrypted during the process. The software does not track your internet history or share your personal details with third parties.

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting Common Issues

If the software does not open, try these steps:

*   Check if you have an active internet connection.
*   Restart your computer if the installer seems to hang.
*   Ensure your antivirus software is not blocking the application.
*   Delete the program and download it again if the file appears corrupt.

If the application fails to find buses, confirm you typed the city names correctly. Spelling errors often result in zero search results. If the problem persists, wait a few minutes and click the "Refresh" button in the search menu. This forces the app to update its link to the travel database.

## 📈 Updating the Software

New versions of the program improve the search speed and add new routes. When an update exists, a notification bar will appear at the top of the main window. Click the "Update" button to refresh your application files. You do not need to uninstall the old version to install the new one. The update process keeps your existing booking records in place. 

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can I book multiple seats at once?
Yes. During the booking step, change the number of passengers in the drop-down menu. The app will adjust the available seats accordingly.

Does the app work offline?
You need an internet connection to book a seat. You can view your saved tickets while offline, but you cannot search for new routes or confirm new bookings.

How do I cancel a booking?
Go to your "My Trips" dashboard. Pick the booking you wish to remove and click "Cancel". The system will update your seat status immediately.
